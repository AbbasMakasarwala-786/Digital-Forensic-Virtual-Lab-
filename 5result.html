Result

In our experiment, we found that volatile data stored in RAM or cache memory is pivotal for forensic investigations. Immediate remote or onsite collection through live forensics is essential, as this data is temporary and susceptible to loss on power interruption. Sustained power supply emerged as a key factor in preserving this crucial information, enabling a deeper understanding of system states during cyber incidents.
